Welcome to Archicheck!
The idea behind Archicheck is to have an architecture that allows users to view and publish critical comments or remarks about the content of any given website in the World Wide Web they're currently on. These comments can either come from other users or already established fact-checking sites you can subscribe to. By subscribing to different fact-checking sites (e.g. correctiv.org), the user has the opportunity to compare their stated facts and check them for potential inequalities.

How to install Archicheck:
Archicheck is a browser extension and can be installed in any browser of your choice(Archicheck was developed for Google Chrome).

How to use:

'Add Fact Checker' (+ button):
Here you can subscribe to a fact checker. Choose a fact checker out of a ranked list. Those will be shown in the list 'Subscribed Fact Checkers'.

'Subscribed Fact Checkers' (middle button):
Here you can see your subscribed Fact Checkers. Click on the trash icon next to a fact checker to unsubscribe.

'Add Fact Check for this website' (right button):
Do a fact check. Choose a fact checker to see if he has published a comment for this website.

'My published Fact Checks' (centered button):
See your publisched fact checks. You can delete each of your fact checks by clicking on the trash icon next to the fact check.

'Settings' (right corner):
Here you can set your preferred settings. You can choose between:
a) 'Show Fact Checks of my subscribed Fact Checkers': You will only see fact checks from your subscribed fact checkers.
b) 'Show Fact Checks of all Fact Checkers': You will see all fact checks for the website that you are browsing on.

'Help' (right corner):
Shows this user guide.

'Profile' (right corner):
Register to Archicheck (if you didn't have) or see your account page.

'Guest Button'
Get into the extension as a guest.
